Welcome to Earnpawa

Welcome to a new world of possibilities where collective success and putting a smile on your face is our major goal. Earnpawa plus Africa initiative (EPP Africa Initiative) is a duo of online and offline membership based empowerment platform with the sole aim of empowering young Africans to succeed financially and in their choice endeavor’s so as to give them the leverage to live the good-life they desire. EPP Africa initiative was born out of necessity for financial freedom, stability, human capacity and entrepreneurial development. It’s true we live in a digital age where limitless life changing innovations and opportunities are easy to come by through the use of the internet; but the many cases of online business inconsistencies and sudden pack up inspired the birth of EPP Africa initiative. The EPP Africa initiative ideology is derived from an African proverb which can be rephrased as "giving a man fish is good, but teaching him how to fish is better" This is so as the foundation on which this platform is created is for total human capacity development for young Africans through Skill acquisition and Entrepreneurship development(SAED). Hence, the SAEDPlus package and other packages available on the platform serves as a means to equip members with skills (digital and vocational) that will make them employable and also fit to employ others. We are here to make life easy and earning online fun for all our participants.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help Africans facilitate access to requisite skills and resources necessary for successful entrepreneurship, financial freedom and self-reliance. Help members grow wealth legitimately while going about their normal activities. With our unique empowerment formula, we hope to provide empowerment and financial opportunities as well as community development services for people to improve their well-being & quality of life.

Our Vision

Our vision is to have an Africa where everybody can experience personal and financial freedom, and live their dream to the fullest. To be an outstanding empowerment platform for economic transformation in Africa and the world. To be a model to other initiatives and those to come aimed at human capacity development.

Our Goals

EPP Africa Initiative is determined to help Africans and our partners worldwide experience personal and financial freedom by enabling solutions that help them discover their potential, develop and activate their employability and entrepreneurship capacity to create value and derive economic benefit.
